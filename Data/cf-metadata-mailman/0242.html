Dear Mike

> Under 2.4, Dimensions, Recommendations, the CF standard says "In files that are meant to conform to the COARDS subset of CF, any dimensions of a variable other than space and time dimensions should be added "to the left" of the space and time dimensions as represented in CDL."

That's only a recommendation, not a requirement. In CF 2.4 it says
"All other dimensions should, whenever possible, be placed to the left of the
spatiotemporal dimensions."
Clearly you have a practical reason for not adhering to this. The
recommendation is made so that files aren't needlessly uncompliant with COARDS.

Cheers
