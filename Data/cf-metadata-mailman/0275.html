Hi John,

Defining terms should be sufficient; terminology is not consistent from
my experience. At some point people my want to extend the basic
terminology. Here is a start:

hindcast - re-creation of past fields using collected observations in
boundary conditions
nowcast - short time-period prediction of near-real-time fields using
real-time data
forecast - prediction of future fields beyond influence of real-time data

-CJ
