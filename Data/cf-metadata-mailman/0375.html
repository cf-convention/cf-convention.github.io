Tom Gross, Steve Hankin, et. al. have bravely volunteered to beta-test
the new CF website discussion boards with their unstructured grid work
(cf-wg-supporting-technologies).
A new mailing list has been created (cf-metadata at lists.llnl.gov) where
messages sent by their discussions will be delivered. As members of
cf-metadata at cgd.ucar.edu, you have automatically been subscribed to this
new list. Please do not send mail directly to the LLNL list or reply to
messages delivered via the message boards. If you want to join the
discussion, you must have an account on the Trac portion of the new CF
website. See this web page for all the details:
 http://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/discussion/about-the-cf-trac-ticket-system/

For CF discussions not related to this working group, continue to use
the normal cf-metadata at cgd.ucar.edu mailing list.

Please fire away with any questions or feedback.

Thanks!
Kyle
