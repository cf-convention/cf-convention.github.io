> Hi John,
>
> If you are specifying wind components then the names x_wind and y_wind
> indicate that the variables are relative to the grid rather than true
> longitude and latitude. The corresponding components relative to true
> lon and lat have the names eastward_wind and northward_wind.
>
> There are also the names wind_from_direction and wind_to_direction for
> velocities relative to lat and lon, but I don't think equivalent grid
> relative names exist at the moment. (I'm sure Jonathan will correct me
> if I am wrong!)
