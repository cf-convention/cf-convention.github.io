Dear Jonathan,

The names for chemical production and loss rates would then remain:

chemical_gross_production_rate_of_X_of_Y
chemical_net_production_rate_of_X_of_Y

For individual reactions the following names could be used:
chemical_production_rate_of_X_of_Y
chemical_ production_rate_of_X_of_Y
with an additional dimension giving the respective reactions.

ok?

Your suggestion also leads to the following names:

unchanged:
surface_dry_deposition_X_flux_of_Y
surface_wet_deposition_X_flux_of_Y
surface_emission_X_flux_of_Y

new:
tendency_of_atmospheric_X_of_Y_due_to_emissions
To be more consistent with the surface flux names, I would suggest:
atmosphere_emission_X_tendency_of_Y

In addition, the surface_emission_X_flux_of_Y
is a subgroup of atmosphere_emission_X_tendency_of_Y
no matter what name we finally choose. Do you agree?

Using
X mass-,number-,mole-concentration/fraction for 3d fields
X mass-,number-,mole-content for vertically integrated 2 d fields
Y species

Best wishes

Christiane
