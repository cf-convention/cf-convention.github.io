We have a good number of people to begin work on a "Point Observation Data" convention. The hob of the working group is to generate a proposal to be presented to the CF committee.

If you are interested, send me an email, then go to

 http://www.unidata.ucar.edu/support/mailinglist/mailing-list-form.html

and subscribe yourself to the cf-pointobsconvention mail list.

All email of the working group will be publicly archived at:

 http://mailman.unidata.ucar.edu/mailing_lists/archives/cf-pointobsconvention/

You will want to be either interested in the nuts and bolts of netcdf and CF, or have point/station/trajectory/profile/etc type data and want to find standard/interoperable ways to write it to disk.
