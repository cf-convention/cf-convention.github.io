CHANGES TO DEFINITIONS OF NAMES
Correct definition of mass_fraction_of_ammonium_dry_aerosol_in_air to
read: ' "Mass fraction of ammonium" means that the mass is expressed as
mass of NH4.'
Add the following sentences to definitions of all names concerning black
carbon aerosol: 'Black carbon aerosol is composed of elemental carbon.
It is strongly light-absorbing.' (This affects just one existing name:
mass_fraction_of_black_carbon_dry_aerosol_in_air).
For sets of names involving:
particulate_organic_matter_dry_aerosol
secondary_particulate_organic_matter_dry_aerosol
primary_particulate_organic_matter_dry_aerosol
amend the definitions to say that the first is the sum of the second and
the third terms. (This affects a triplet of existing names - the
mass_fraction names for these aerosols).
