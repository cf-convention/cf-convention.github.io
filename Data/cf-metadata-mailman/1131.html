Dear Olivier

> ocean mean dynamic topography.
Do you mean a time-mean? If so, this can be described by cell_methods as
well,
by including "TIME: mean", where TIME is the name of the time dimension.

> ? ocean_vertical_eddy_diffusivity, m2/s
I think we will need to be careful about what "eddy" means. Can you clarify
what you imply by "eddy"?

> ? sea_ice_x_velocity_where_sea_ice , m/s
> ? sea_ice_y_velocity_where_sea_ice, m/s
> ? sea_ice_area_fraction_where_sea_ice, no units
> ? sea_ice_thickness_where_sea_ice, m
> ? sea_ice_downward_x_stress_where_sea_ice, Pa
> ? sea_ice_downward_y_stress_where_sea_ice , Pa

These "where" quantities would be unnecessary in my proposal
http://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/trac/ticket/17
because the "where" part would be included in cell_methods.
