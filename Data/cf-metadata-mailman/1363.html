Hi Ethan,

An obvious one perhaps, but one which we may find increasingly useful /
relevant in light of the rise and rise of GoogleEarth-type applications,
is the Orthographic projection. This being the classic 'view from space'
projection.

Same grid mapping parameters as the other azimuthal projections, viz.

grid_mapping_name = "orthographic"

longitude_of_projection_origin
latitude_of_projection_origin
false_easting
false_northing

(though off the top of my head I can't think of a scenario where one
would want to use the last two params).

Regards,
Phil
