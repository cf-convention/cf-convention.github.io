I propose that the standard name "area" be added. It is needed for the
next round of CMIP (CMIP5), where we will collect the grid cell areas
for each model grid.

to make a correction to the CF documentation. Currently Example 7.3
includes a line that reads:

  'cell_area:standard_name="area";'
