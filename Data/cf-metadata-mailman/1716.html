Dear colleagues,

There is obviously a very active discussion on the best strategy to
construct new standard names for aerosols and chemicals at the CF
mailing list. Therefore, I try to summarize the current status of work
on a wiki page in order to highlight the principles and concepts we have
developed so far.

http://wiki.esipfed.org/index.php/CF_Standard_Names_-_Construction_of_Atmospheric_Chemistry_and_Aerosol_Terms

Please note that the discussions of the aerosol and chemisty names in CF
should be continued on the CF mailing list, and not on this wiki. The
wiki is only used to summarize the state of such names in CF. The
descriptions on the wiki could help to converge to the most efficient
strategy. (Please note, that Modifications of existing CF standard names
are suggested, if they do not fit in the general concepts.)

A discussion on a more general approach to standard name construction is
under discussion at the moment (Thread "a different (but perhaps
unoriginal) approach to standard name construction" launched by Karl
Taylor.)

Best regards,
Christiane
