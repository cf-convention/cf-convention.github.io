Dear Anna

I think the problem is that udunits thinks g is a unit of acceleration!
However, it will accept gram and milligram, so you could try that instead.

$ udunits
You have: g
You want: m s-2
    <m s-2> = <g>*9.80665
    <m s-2> = <g>/0.101972
You have: milligram
You want: kg
    <kg> = <milligram>*1e-06
    <kg> = <milligram>/1e+06

Cheers

Jonathan
