Dear Olivier ,

I have one short remark concerning the "expressed_as_X" names: The
expressed_as_X should be next to the species, e.g.:

mass_concentration_of_organic_detritus_expressed_as_nitrogen_in_sea_water

and not

mass_concentration_of_organic_detritus_in_sea_water_expressed_as_nitrogen

Cheers,
Christiane
