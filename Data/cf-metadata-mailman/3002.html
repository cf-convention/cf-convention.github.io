Dear colleagues,

    we just posted a summary of "CF problems" which were encountered in the TFHTAP multi-model initiative and their fixes on the HTAP Wiki: http://htap.icg.fz-juelich.de/data/CFAdaption (or go to http://htap.icg.fz-juelich.de/ then navigate to "HTAP Model and Experiment Descriptions" and then click on "Adaption of HTAP data to CF standard 1.0 (WCS)"). We hope that you find this illustrative as a "community of practice" example and that the TFHTAP modellers who will be involved in other intercomparison activities can make use of the information and shell scripts provided in order to ease the burden for those people who attempt to analyze such multi-model data sets.
