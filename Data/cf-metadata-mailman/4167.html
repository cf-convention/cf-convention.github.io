> Dear Randy
>
> > In the problem domain I am working, there are multiple related data
> > variables in the same coordinate space for which the quality flags
> > are the same. It would be a stretch to call these multiple related
> > data variables "vectors" in the true mathematical sense.
>
> > Given this looser definition of "vector", the notion of having
> > standard names associated with the umbrella data variable does not
> > seem to make sense as different projects would potentially have
> > project-unique groupings of variables where it is desirable to share
> > quality flags, etc.
>
> I agree. The discussions of Thomas's umbrella variable have touched on
> this
> point but it doesn't seem to suit the purpose of providing a single
> quality
> flag variable for an arbitrary selection of geophysical quantities.
> Hence I
> think the idea of generalising the standard_name att to the format
> standard_name [standard_name ...] modifier
> i.e. permitting more than two words in it would be more suitable and
> general-purpose. The main motivation for the umbrella variable is
> different
> viz. to identify a group of data variables which together constitute a
> single
> higher-level geophysical quantity.
>
> Best wishes
