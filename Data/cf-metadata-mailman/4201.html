To the NetCDF-CF Metadata Working Group:

I am a co-chair of the Global Emissions Initiative
(GEIA,http://www.geiacenter.org/) and a lead on the Community Initiative
for Emissions Research and Applications (CIERA, http://ciera-air.org/).
On behalf of GEIA and CIERA, I would like to request that you take up
consideration of a proposal put forth by Martin Schultz of FZ Juelich
for new CF standard names for emissions (Martin's message is included
below).

Emissions inventories are of critical importance to Earth system
modeling. To be useful, these complex data sets must be made accessible
to the broad community of scientists who need them. Objective evaluation
of inventory datasets is also critical, and requires systematic efforts
to make use of the wide variety of observational and modeling data that
are available on the web.

Martin has been a leader in promoting the interoperability of
atmospheric chemistry datasets and designing web systems for these data.
His proposal for standard emissions names in netCDF-CF is key to making
inventory datasets accessible by existing data portals and usable by
online applications. Fully interoperable emissions data are absolutely
essential if GEIA and CIERA are to realize their vision of increasing
access to and improving emissions information.

I respectfully request that your working group act on Martin's proposal
as soon as possible.

Sincerely,
Greg Frost
