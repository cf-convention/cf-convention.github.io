Dear Heiko,

In response to your point about trac, the current practice is to for standard names proposals to be made on the mailing list and discussed here. The trac system is used to discuss proposals for changes to the conventions document.

Thank you for your comments about the sectors. I am not sufficiently expert to comment on how the sectors themselves should be defined, so further discussion of this point would be helpful.

The syntax 'due_to_emission_from_source' is already in use in a number of standard names. This was suggested so as to avoid having 'due_to_process_due_to_source' which would certainly be confusing. I think that emission is the process that is changing the atmospheric abundance of the species and that from_source is a way of being more specific about where the emission came from. Personally, I like this approach and I think the meaning of the names is clear.

Best wishes,
Alison
