Dear Heiko,

     you are of course right that this cannot be the end of it. Yet, I think it is very important to get these standard names into the list soon, because there are already large data sets with these categories (the "ACCMIP" emissions produced for chemistry calculations to support IPCC AR5, and "MACCity" emissions which are employed in the quasi-operational MACC forecasts and reanalyses). The issue of sector definitions had been discussed and in the end the majority view was that we should follow the practical approach and adopt what is already in use. Nevertheless, the emissions community should indeed follow up on this and create a more comprehensive vocabulary. A good opportunity to carry this forward might be the next GEIA conference in Toulouse in mid June.

Best regards,

Martin
