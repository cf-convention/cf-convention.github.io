Jonathan,
We thought about using "1', but Paragraph 3.1 in CF 1.6 states "1" can be used for fractions or parts of a whole. Reflectance factor is neither a fraction nor part of a whole so I think the better option is Jim's suggestion - no units attribute, unless we're missing something about the uses of "1".
Mike
