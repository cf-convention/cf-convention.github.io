Hello CF community

I am interested in taking the excellent work on a logical data model for CF Jonathan and David documented in https://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/trac/ticket/68 further, in the hope that this may reach a stable, agreed state.

I would like to address the scope and terms of reference of the logical data model first, as I feel that this sets the scene and helps us all to consider how to input into detail discussions and what the implications of adopting a logical data model are.

I have thus created a new ticket
https://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/trac/ticket/88

focussed exclusively on agreeing the terms of reference and scope

I encourage people to look at this and consider the implications. I would like to try and get agreement on the these so enable a continuation of the discussions on the data model details.

I hope that this will allow the community to consider adopting a CF data model as part of the CF metadata project.

all the best
mark
