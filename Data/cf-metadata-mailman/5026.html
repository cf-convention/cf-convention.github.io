Dear All,

The standard name table has been updated on the CF website (http://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/documents/cf-standard-names). The current version is now version 22 dated 12 February 2013. The changes have also been published on the NERC vocabulary server.

Angelika Heil and Randy Horne have been added to the list of standard name contributors (http://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/documents/cf-standard-names/cf-standard-name-contributors). (This month's other contributors are already listed).

The standard name table will be updated again in approximately one month's time. Names that are formally accepted in the intervening period will be included in the next update.

Best wishes,
Alison

> 2. Clarification to definitions of existing names
>
> a. toa_bidirectional_reflectance.
>
> The definition will be updated following discussion of a question asked by
> Randy Horne. The following text will be added to clarify the meaning:
> ' toa_bidirectional_reflectance includes a factor to account for the cosine of
> the solar zenith angle but does not include any integration over solid angle.'
>
> b.
> tendency_of_atmosphere_mass_content_of_X_due_to_emission_from_ind
> ustrial_processes_and_combustion names. Proposed by martin Schultz and
> Angelika Heil.
>
> 22 names of this pattern were recently added to the standard name table
> (version 20, 12 September 2012). The definitions contained a typo in the list
> of IPCC emissions categories: category 2E, "Production of halocarbons SF6",
> is being replaced by category 2G " Non-energy use of lubricants/waxes
> (CO2)".
