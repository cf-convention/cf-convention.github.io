Folks: we have a fire product represented as a set of variables storing
gridded data at a particular horizontal spatial resolution. The product is
composed of several data variables: (1) a fire code value, (2) temperature
of the fire within the cells where fires exist, (3) radiative power of the
fire within cells where the fires exist, and (4) the area of the fire
within the cells where fires exist, which may be less than the cell's area.
  In this case, does it make sense to establish a "cell_measures"
relationship between (2) temperature variable and/or (3) power variable,
and (4) the fire area variable ? very respectfully, randy
