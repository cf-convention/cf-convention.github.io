Hi all,

and sorry for cross-posting... the ODIP program was brought to my attention
today. I had never heard of it, and felt like sharing this with you in case
you are as ignorant as I am...

This is an EU-funded initiative, in collaboration with other international
partners esp. in the US and Australia, to make oceanographic data holdings
more compatible with each other.

For further details, please see their website:

http://www.odip.eu

If you feel you have something to contribute or to learn, I suppose now would
be a good time to contact them.
