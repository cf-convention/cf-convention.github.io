Dear CF board,

We have an algorithm for aerosol optical depth which yields statistics
over oceans, strictly.

strictly synonymous with
'ocean,' or is it more inclusive, e.g., large lakes. If 'sea' is indeed
meant to be a rather
inclusive term, we would like to propose the addition of 'ocean' to the
table to give a more
precise context to our application.

It may also be worthwhile to provide a definition for 'sea,' if it is
indeed intended to be more encompassing
that 'ocean.'
