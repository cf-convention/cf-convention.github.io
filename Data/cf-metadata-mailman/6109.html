If my auxiliary coordinate is measured, as opposed to computed (in a trajectory or swath, for example), I may very well have a situation where I have measurements of data where I don?t have a measurement of location. If I wish to preserve the data measurements, I need to able to have fill in my auxiliary coordinate variables without fill in my data variables. How will I handle that given the proposed limitations?
