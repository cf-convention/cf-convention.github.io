On 12/03/14 17:41, Jeffrey F. Painter wrote:
> You may have gathered that I don't think the highlighting system has
> worked as well as originally intended, so I would welcome a change -
> whether or not we continue to use DocBook, etc.

As someone that reasonably often consults the conventions documents, I
find the highlighting/deletion of provisional stuff makes the documents
quite hard to read after a few changes. While I see some value in
knowing what parts are new/provisional, I agree with Jeff and would
happily support a change that made it easier to read, either by dropping
this requirement, "deprovisionalising" more quickly or finding an
alternative way to do this.

Cheers,
