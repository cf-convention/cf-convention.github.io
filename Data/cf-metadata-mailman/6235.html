Dear CF Board,

I would like to propose a new standard name:

Standard_Name:
radius_of_tropical_cyclone_maximum_sustained_winds

Definition:
The great circle distance measured from the tropical cyclone center to
the region of sustained
1-minute duration maximum wind speed, as defined by the standard name,
tropical_cyclone_maximum_sustained_wind_speed.

Canonical_Units: meter
