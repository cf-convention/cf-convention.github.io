Thank you for the reply.

To summarize this proposed standard name, then:

Standard_Name:
radius_of_tropical_cyclone_defined_by_maximum_sustained_wind_speed

Canonical units: m

Definition:
The great circle distance measured from the tropical cyclone center to
the region of sustained 1-minute duration
maximum wind speed, as defined by the standard name,
tropical_cyclone_maximum_sustained_wind_speed.

Gary
