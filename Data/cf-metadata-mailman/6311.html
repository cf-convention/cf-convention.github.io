We are currently developing software for the GOCE
mission, called GUT:

conventions and therefore try to use the standard names approved.?

following quantities:

Ocean Kinetic Energy
Ocean Relative
Vorticity (vertical component)

We would like to suggest the

therefore we kindly request your feedback.
