Seems a shame that this wasn't originally handled with a standard name
of 'vertical_air_velocity' (or some such) that required a 'positive'
attribute. Both upward and downward could be covered with no confusion.

On 9/11/14, 11:52 AM, Jonathan Gregory wrote:
> Dear Ken
>
>> I would like to propose a new standard_name to complement upward_air_velocity. upward_air_velocity uses positive values to indicate upward direction, but we have many instrument that use a convention of positive values to indicate downward direction. Can we clone upward_air_velocity to downward_air_velocity with the sign convention reversed?
> I would say Yes! There are many such pairs, for just that reason. The
> motivation for this approach is that it is impossible to say what the
> quantity is without also making the sign convention explicit, which is
> a benefit to analysts.
>
