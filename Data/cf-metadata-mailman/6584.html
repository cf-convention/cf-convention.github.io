Hello Jonathan

This topic is not related to CF#108 (which I need to get back to and consider in detail).

This is an explicit piece of metadata which is provided by some systems and models and is applied to the ensemble collection, rather than statistics derived from that collection.

In most cases there will be no CellMethod but there will be a realization dimension and coordinate.

This piece of metadata could be considered to be:
  'The expected length of the realization coordinate and dimension, if the whole ensemble collection is present.'

This is preserved during subset operations, so that I may be looking one ensemble member, with a realization coordinate value of 4, but I know that this is from an ensemble of 17 members.

This is useful metadata which is available from some models and we would like to preserve it in a standardised way in our CF NetCDF data sets.

I hope this clarifies the question

all the best
mark

Dear Mark

Is this related to ticket 108? In that ticket, you and I have very similar
proposals, while Karl has made a more general proposal. In our simple
proposal, the number of realizations would the size of the collapsed axis,
and you could have a cell_methods entry for the collapsed axis such as
  realization: mean (dimension: members)
where "realization" is allowed because it's a standard_name, and "members"
is the name of a dimension which contains the original size of the ensemble
before collapse. This would work in my version of the proposal because it
does not require any coordinate vars to exist with this dimension (though they
could if you want to supply them too).

Best wishes

Jonathan
