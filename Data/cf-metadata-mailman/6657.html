Dear all

I am very pleased to announce that John Graybeal has compiled a CF FAQ, which
is now linked to the CF website http://cfconventions.org, or directly at
http://cfconventions.org/faq.html. I believe this will be a great resource for
users and developers of CF and I would like to thank John for his hard work on
this on behalf of the community. I am sure he will be pleased to receive
comments and suggestions. I expect that as questions come up on the email list,
especially if they have been frequently asked, the answers will be added to the
FAQ, and thus it will be become increasingly useful as a first place to look.

Thanks also to Matthew Harris for installing it, and for his and Jeff Painter's
continuing work on migrating the CF documents to the new website. Most of the
content is there now.

Cheers

Jonathan
