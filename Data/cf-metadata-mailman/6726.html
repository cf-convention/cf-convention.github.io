I would like propose 2 new standard names,

- sea_surface_swell_wave_from_direction

- sea_surface_wind_wave_from_direction
to complement the existing standard names for wave directions. The *_from_direction and *_to_direction direction names already exist both total waves (sea_surface_wave_from_direction, sea_surface_wave_to_direction)
Yet for the wind and swell parts only the *_to_direction version exists (sea_surface_swell_wave_to_direction and sea_surface_wind_wave_to_direction).
