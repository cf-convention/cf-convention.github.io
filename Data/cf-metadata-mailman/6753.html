Hi Sean,

You can't make depth into a dimension unless it's one-dimensional and
monotonic, which would give it a one-to-one correspondence with
model_level_number. It sounds like that's not the case here.

I believe what you want to do is to declare depth as an auxiliary
coordinate variable by adding the name of the depth variable to the
"coordinates" attribute of your data variables for currents. Sections 5,
5.2 and 6.2 of the CF spec cover auxiliary coordinates.

That ought to be sufficient for depth to be automatically included when
a user selects one of the currents as an output parameter. (And if it's
not, I would say that means the data portal software doesn't understand
CF very well, and needs some fixing up.)

Cheers,
