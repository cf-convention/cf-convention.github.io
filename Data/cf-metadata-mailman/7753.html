Dear Karl

I think that allowing strings as an alternative to char arrays is a natural
extension and should not be problematic since the information content is the
same. It's good to hear from Jim that the cf-checker is happy with it. We
have discussed this before. As you say, the CF convention does not allow for
it at the moment, and so a change is needed. This depends on someone carefully
going through the document to see what might need to be changed to include
this alternative.

Best wishes

Jonathan
