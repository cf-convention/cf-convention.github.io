Dear Karl

I would not do this in an ideal world, because it means we're creating two ways
of doing the same thing, but I do not have a violent objection. I agree with
you and Tim that it is an easy and economical solution. As he says, appropriate
guidance is needed. I think the current standard is unclear about whether it
is allowed to have missing data in vertices (my interpretation/assumption was
that it isn't allowed) so we need a defect ticket at least to say explicitly
that it is allowed. As well as that, once simple geometries are introduced, we
should comment that that approach is more flexible and economical so should be
preferred if there is a wide range of numbers of vertices.

Best wishes

Jonathan
