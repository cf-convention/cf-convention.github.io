Thank you Daniel for mentioning the draft CF2-Group initiative.

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KK6IZ2ZmpaUTVgrw-GlFd6almppjvGz6D7nxVTO3BtI/edit

Here is a brief update on CF2-Group:

1. The draft is part of the "CF2" effort.
2. It is compatible with most existing NASA and ESA satellite
    datasets, including S5P/TROPOMI mentioned by Maarten.
    It also supports ensembles, mentioned by Jim and David.
3. NCO fully supports it (S5P-testing identified some issues that
    we fix in the forthcoming NCO 4.7.4).
4. It was discussed, revised, and approved at the CF2 meeting in
    ~September and by NASA DIWG in ~December. However it is still a
    Draft document, and we expect more comments and feedback once
    we post it to the CF-list.
5. We will post it to the CF-list in tandem with a related CF2
    proposal (for swath data) once NASA DIWG votes on that, or by
    May 1, whichever comes first.

Comments and feedback are most welcome on the document.
