Dear All,

The standard name table has been updated and the current version is now version 66, dated 15th May 2019. A KWIC index for version 66 has been added to the CF website and all changes have also been published on the NERC vocabulary server.

The next update will take place in June, probably on the 17th, although the exact date is still TBC.

Best wishes,
Alison

3. Modifications to definitions of existing names
a. Area fraction names
Martin Juckes and Karl Taylor proposed adding the following to the definitions of all area_fraction names:
' "Area fraction" is the fraction of a grid cell's horizontal area that has some characteristic of interest. It is evaluated as the area of interest divided by the grid cell area. It may be expressed as a fraction, a percentage, or any other dimensionless representation of a fraction.'

c. Forcing and radiative effect names
During the discussion of Martin's proposals for improving consistency it was agreed to add the following to the definitions of all radiative forcing and radiative effect names:
'A positive radiative forcing or radiative effect is equivalent to a downward radiative flux and contributes to a warming of the earth system.'

d. Parametric coordinates
A number of standard names refer to Appendix D of the conventions document in their defintions. At CF version 1.7 the title of this appendix changed from "Dimensionless vertical coordinates" to "Parametric vertical coordinates". The standard name definitions have been updated to reflect this.
