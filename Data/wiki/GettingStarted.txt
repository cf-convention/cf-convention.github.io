== Getting Started with Unstructured Grids Discussion Group ==

First join the CF mailing list:  
 1. Request a Trac account by sending your first name, last name, and preferred username to: webmaster@pcmdi.llnl.gov.  See also <a href="http://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/discussion/about-the-cf-trac-ticket-system/">CF Trac System</a>.
 1. Subscribe to the unstructured grid mailing list: send a message to majordomo@lists.llnl.gov with the following contained in the email body: 
{{{
subscribe cf-wg-supporting-technologies
end
}}}
