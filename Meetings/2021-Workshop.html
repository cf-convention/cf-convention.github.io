2021 CF Workshop

The 2021 CF Workshop will be a virtual meeting on 21-23 September 2021. The meeting will run for three hours on each day, 15:00 to 18:00 UTC, plus an informal hour at the end of each day.

Thanks for its support of this meeting to:

  • IS-ENES3 which is funded by the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under grant agreement No 824084
  • Institute of Physics of Cantabria (IFCA, CSIC-UC)
