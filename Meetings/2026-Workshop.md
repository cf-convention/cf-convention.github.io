# 2026 CF Workshop

The 2026 CF workshop will be a hybrid event hosted both online and at ECMWF, Bonn, Germany from Monday 21st to Thursday 24th September 2026.

## Registration

Participants are encouraged to register early, no later than 14 September.

Further practical information about the workshop, including the registration link, can be found at the [ECMWF CF Workshop page](https://events.ecmwf.int/event/568/).

## Agenda

To be confirmed.

The workshop aims to foster collaboration across the international CF Conventions community, support the evolution and adoption of CF metadata standards, and promote developments in CF-compliant data. It will also facilitate discussion on interoperability across data formats and systems, while enabling practical progress through collaborative hackathons and working groups. 

In this year’s workshop, some of the topics we will focus on are: 
* GRIB to CF 
* Applying CF to different data formats 
* Encoding uncertainty metadata in CF

Additional topic suggestions are very welcome.

Participants will engage in a combination of plenary sessions, invited talks, and interactive discussions, alongside hands-on hackathons and breakout sessions.

If you have ideas or want to contribute, please communicate this the following GitHub discussions:
* [Presentations](https://github.com/orgs/cf-convention/discussions/475)
* [Breakout and hackathon sessions](https://github.com/orgs/cf-convention/discussions/476)
