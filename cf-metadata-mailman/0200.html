> One question on situation (b): You make both one-dimensional auxiliary
> coordinate variables, but could you make one a coordinate and one an
> auxiliary coordinate? Eg:

Coordinate variables have to be monotonic, and this will not generally be
true in the cases where you have, in effect, a 2D (possibly sparse) set of
combinations of analysis and validity times. That is the reason for using
an index axis, with both times as auxiliaries.
