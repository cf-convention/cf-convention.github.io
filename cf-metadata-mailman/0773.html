'Concern' is not the word I would use, but it is certainly something that should not be ignored. I need an operational system now (NDG and SeaDataNet have delivery deadlines in 6/12 months respectively) and can make something work based on URLs. However, whilst doing this I am keeping a very close eye on the activities of Simon Cox/Rob Atkinson in MOTIIVE and the OGC arena developing feature type catalogue registry technology in which URNs are the currency. So, when I make the technology transition I plan to make the transition in the nature of my URIs.
