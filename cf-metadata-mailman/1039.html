I am contacting you on behave of the conveners of the session IN12:
"Building Community and Governance within Earth and Space Science
Content Models" at the next AGU Fall meeting (10?14 December 2007,

We are thinking that it would be extremely valuable if you share how
the community building and governance is handle within CF and within
the atmospheric community. We hope that we can start to work out
better coordination and governance of the standards that are being
developed in earth and space systems science.

you are planning to attend AGU and give a presentation or if someone
else from CF could present.
