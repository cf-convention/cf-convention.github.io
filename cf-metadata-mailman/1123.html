Dear Allison and Christiane (and all, of course),

    I like Allison's suggestion to remove the "all" from the name and expand the description. The "group" idea was based on precisely the opposite, namely to indicate for example for lumped NMHCs that this is something that is not consistently defined among models. You might argue that this means it shouldn't be a standard name then, but I would say the similarities are still enough to merit such a definition. The "group" part of the name would however slaert the user that there is something he/she needs to look out for. In that sense I feel that "all" is perhaps to inclusive and indicates more of an agreement than there may be.

    .. could be, though, that I exaggerated the "group" concept a bit in my first email on this subject.
