On Thu, 2008-10-16 at 13:33 +0100, Pamment, JA (Alison) wrote:
> Dear All,
>
> I am planning to update the standard name table on Tuesday 21st October
> to add a number of names that have been agreed in recent months. Please
> see below for the list of planned changes. If anyone spots any errors
> in the list, please contact me as soon as possible. On this occasion
> there will also be some modification to the "Guidelines for Construction
> of CF Standard Names"
> (http://cf-pcmdi.llnl.gov/documents/cf-standard-names/guidelines).
>
> Another update of the standard name table is scheduled for 11th
> November.
>
> 1. Aliases to be introduced for existing standard names.
>
> water_flux_into_ocean will become water_flux_into_sea_water; kg m-2 s-1
>
> In addition, the description of this name will be extended slightly to
> mention "relaxation". Thus, the definition will now read:
> "Water" means water in all phases. The water flux into the ocean is the
> freshwater entering the sea water as a result of precipitation,
> evaporation, river inflow, sea ice effects and water flux relaxation and
> correction (if applied). In accordance with common usage in geophysical
> disciplines, "flux" implies per unit area, called "flux density" in
> physics.
>
> water_flux_into_ocean_from_rivers will become
> water_flux_into_sea_water_from_rivers; kg m-2 s-1
> water_volume_transport_into_ocean_from_rivers will become
> water_volume_transport_into_sea_water_from_rivers; m3 s-1
> wind_mixing_energy_flux_into_ocean will become
> wind_mixing_energy_flux_into_sea_water; W m-2
>
