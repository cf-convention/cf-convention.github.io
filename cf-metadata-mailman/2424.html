Ingeborg Noehren and I'd like to propose 2 new standard_names:
Sea_surface_wave_skewness
Sea surface wave skewness of the directional energy distribution per
frequency D() is obtained from second order and third order circular
moments of D().
Unit: [1]
Sea_surface_wave_kurtosis
Sea surface wave kurtosis of the directional energy distribution per
frequency D() is obtained from second order and fourth order circular
moments of D().
Unit: [1]
