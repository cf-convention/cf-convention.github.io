I suggest filing a request with Udunits support to restore "bits" as a
valid unit in Udunits-2. It was probably dropped by accident.
All unit strings from Udunits-1 should be inherited, with very limited
and documented exceptions.

http://www.unidata.ucar.edu/software/udunits/udunits-2/udunits2.html#Dif
