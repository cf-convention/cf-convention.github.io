Dear Jonathan,

Just to summarize, then, the proposed standard name would thus be
tropical_cyclone_eye_brightness_temperature

Units: Celsius

Definition:
"tropical_cyclone_eye_brightness_temperature" means the warmest
brightness temperature value in the eye region
of a tropical cyclone (0 - 24 km from the storm center) derived using
the Advanced Dvorak Technique, based on
satellite observations.

Thanks and regards,

Gary
