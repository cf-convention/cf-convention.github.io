Dear colleagues,

On behalf of the netCDF-CF 2019 workshop organising committee I?d like to draw your attention to the workshop agenda. The workshop will be taking place from Tue 16 Jul 2019 ? Wed 17 Jul 2019 in four sessions, and the agenda is as described on the ESIP pages:

  * Session 1: Welcome<https://sched.co/PtPD>
  * Session 2: Governance and evolutions<https://sched.co/PxPX>
  * Session 3: Recent / current proposals<https://sched.co/PtPV>
  * Session 4: Other business and wrap-up<https://sched.co/PtPn>

We?d be happy to see as many CFers taking part as are available and interested, either in person if you can or remotely. Session details are available on the pages. If you?d like to take responsibility for a time slot or propose a change, don?t hesitate to get in touch.

In addition to the cut-and-dry work described in the agenda, there will be plenty of time for talk, collaboration, heated discussion, etc. in breaks and side sessions. We?ll be organising an informal workshop dinner on the first day (Tuesday) ? time and place are TBD. Get in touch with me or any of the other organisers if you?re interested in coming.

Looking forward to seeing you there!
